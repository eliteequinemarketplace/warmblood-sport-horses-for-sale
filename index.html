Warmblood sport horses are the backbone of hunter jumper competition in North America and around the world. Purpose-bred by leading European studbooks for natural movement, scope, and trainability, warmbloods consistently produce the top hunters, jumpers, equitation horses, and derby horses competing at USEF-rated shows across the country. Elite Equine Marketplace lists warmblood sport horses from trusted breeders, trainers, and sellers worldwide — from young prospects and developing horses through established show horses with documented competition records.

KWPN — Dutch Warmblood

The KWPN studbook (Koninklijk Warmbloed Paardenstamboek Nederland) is widely considered the premier warmblood breeding program in the world for hunter jumper sport. KWPN horses are known for their exceptional movement — a naturally rhythmic, ground-covering trot and canter — combined with a careful, scopey jump and an intelligent, trainable temperament. The Netherlands produces both hunter-type and jumper-type KWPN horses, with the hunter type prized for its flat, flowing jump and natural hunter way of going, and the jumper type valued for its genuine scope, bravery, and athletic power. Many of the top hunter and jumper horses in North America carry KWPN breeding.

Belgian Warmblood — BWP

The BWP (Belgian Warmblood) studbook has produced some of the most successful Grand Prix show jumpers in the world, with a strong emphasis on athletic scope, careful technique, and competitive durability. BWP horses are a popular choice for US jumper programs at every level — from amateur-friendly 1.10m horses through FEI Grand Prix competitors. Belgian Warmbloods are also increasingly represented in the US hunter and equitation rings, where their natural rideability and trainable temperament make them versatile performers.

Hanoverian — Hannoveraner Verband

Hanoverian horses from Germany's Hannoveraner Verband are among the most classical and elegant warmbloods in the sport. Known for their correct conformation, expressive movement, and balanced gaits, Hanoverians excel in the hunter and equitation rings where movement quality is paramount. Hanoverian hunters are prized for their naturally expressive trot, a floating, reaching canter, and a beautiful jump style — qualities that win consistently in the hunter and equitation divisions.

Oldenburg, Selle Francais & Other European Breeds

Beyond the major studbooks, Elite Equine Marketplace lists warmblood sport horses from a range of European breeding programs. Oldenburg horses from Germany combine scope and power with elegant movement. Selle Francais horses from France are renowned for their athleticism, bravery, and competitive fire — producing world-class show jumpers and derby horses. Irish Sport Horses blend Thoroughbred energy and heart with warmblood scope and rideability. Elite Equine Marketplace welcomes quality sport horses from all backgrounds and breeding programs.

Young Warmblood Prospects for Sale

Young warmblood prospects — typically 3 to 6 year old horses with studbook registration, inspection scores, and the natural qualities to develop into competitive hunter jumper horses — represent some of the best value in the sport horse market. Elite Equine Marketplace lists young warmblood prospects from reputable European and North American breeders, with studbook documentation, inspection scores where available, and video of movement and early training.
